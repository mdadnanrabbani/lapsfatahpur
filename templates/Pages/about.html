Vision & Mission

Our guiding statements shape everything we do — from classroom practices to community engagement.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant learning community that empowers students to excel academically, grow ethically, and lead meaningfully in a changing world.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, inclusive, and stimulating environment that nurtures curiosity, creativity, collaboration, and character — aligned with CBSE’s learner-centric approach.

Core Values & Philosophy

Integrity

We act with honesty and fairness, celebrating transparency in all actions.

Respect

We value diverse voices and cultivate empathy, kindness, and mutual trust.

Excellence

Pursuing high standards in academics, sports, arts, and co‑curriculars.

Curiosity

Inquiry-based learning that sparks discovery and problem-solving.

Collaboration

We learn as a community — students, teachers, and parents together.

Responsibility

We build responsible citizens with strong ethics and social awareness.

History of the School

2005

LAPS founded with a vision to offer quality CBSE education in Fatahpur.

Inaugural batch welcomed into a humble yet spirited campus.

2012

New academic block and science labs established.

Integration of ICT tools into classrooms; digital smart boards introduced.

2018

Sports complex and library expansion completed.

Clubs launched for robotics, arts, and community projects.

2024

Campus modernization and blended learning initiatives rolled out.

Parent engagement app and ERP adopted for seamless communication.
